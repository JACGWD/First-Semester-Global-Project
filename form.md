# Building the Contact Form

**Emmet Code:**

    form>fieldset>legend+(label+input:text)*2+label+input:tel^select>option*4^fieldset>legend+label+textarea^div.align-right>input:reset+input:submit


The illustration below shows the form code properly completed with all values:

![Commented HTML form code](./img/commented-form.png)


Please customize the fields of the form according to the requirements of your
project\'s type of business or activity. See the [W3Schools page on
forms](https://www.w3schools.com/tags/tag_form.asp) and on [form
elements](https://www.w3schools.com/html/html_form_elements.asp) for
more info.

For example, a restaurant might use the select dropdown menu to offer available times for reservations. Or a clothing shop might display the range of available colors for a product via the select dropdown menu. Think of what is logical for your client.
